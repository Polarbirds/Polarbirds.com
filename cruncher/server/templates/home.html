How the thing works

A stat is used in games, these are what the players will use to keep track of their games.

A formula is a sort of recipe for games. The players will create formulas and use them in their games. A formula is defined by a list of stats

A game consists of a formula and a list of players. It also has two variables that are the time when the game starts and when it ends

All games, players and formulas haves ids, so that they can be accessed and reviewed after their creation.
